The Creamy Entertainment System

> Ever since I attended my first QuakeCon, I was fascinated by the different designs of the case mods some of the attendees brought in. I thought about building my own computer, but didn't actually start one until November of 2003. My first custom-built computer is a compact design, with somewhat of a gaming console feel. I call my build the Creamy Entertainment System, a console-like name with my personal touch.

Written Log - A very detailed report of my build. Perhaps a bit too detailed. You decide.
