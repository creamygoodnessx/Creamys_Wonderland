MUGEN Tournaments

> Once every few years, I gather a bunch MUGEN characters from all around the Internet, and pit them against each other in AI only tournaments. The trees and scoreboards for each tournament, as well as links to the competing characters, can be found in the MUGEN / AI Tournaments section under Games.
