QuakeCon 2001 - Closing

> As for Day Four... well, for me there was no Day Four. We checked out of the Comfort and took a plane back to New Jersey that morning. Despite our expectations, my parents actually enjoyed the vacation, as they got a chance to go to downtown Dallas and do some shopping. In the end, I got several things out of this trip, including two T-shirts, a beta copy of the Generations Arena mod, several media files from the network, including a few good mp3s, some LA Blue Girl episodes, and a DivX of Monty Python And The Holy Grail, and a bumper sticker. But I also got some great things that were non-tangable; a chance to meet the Polycount crew in person, and the knowledge on how to finally fix Quake 3 Arena on Candybox. Sorry to bore you with this long and useless story, but I feel it was something I needed to get off my chest.
