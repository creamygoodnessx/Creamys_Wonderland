QuakeCon 2014 - Day Two

> I continued playing Hedgewars past midnight, and then went up to my hotel room to initially retired for the night around 12:45AM, although I had to return to the BYOC room a little later to pick up some clothes that I planned to wear for the second day of the QuakeCon. After flossing and brushing my teeth, I went to bed some time after 2AM. After sunrise, I got out of bed at 8AM, showered, put on a new set of clothes including my deadmau5 T-shirt, and left the room about an hour later to return to CES in the BYOC room. I spent the morning playing SuperTuxKart on CES, and looking around the BYOC. Besides exploring the BYOC room, I went next door to the vendor's area and checked that out as well. One of the booths in the vendor's area was the Modders-Inc_booth, and I entered their annual case mod contest just before 11AM. After filling out a short form, I was given a cardboard marker with the number 22 printed on it, and was asked to place it on top of my machine. When I asked the person at Modder's Inc. when the judging would take place, he said that the judges would be going around the BYOC room and checking out the contestants' machines sometime on Saturday morning. After leaving the vendor's booth, I went back to CES and put the cardboard indicator just behind CES.

> After entering the case mod contest, I went up to Room 514 to have a slice of pizza and a Cinnistick around noon, then came back down to the BYOC room around 1:30PM. I played a few more rounds of Quake 2 on Tastyspleen's DM server, played some more Hedgewars, and then got up to check out some of the case mods in the BYOC room, some of which were entering in the case mod contest. Then, I went up to my hotel room sometime after 4PM to have another slice of pizza, and then returned to the BYOC room around 7:30PM. Upon my return, I downloaded and installed the Stronghold: On The Edge Of Chaos mod for Doom 2, bought a grape flavored medium shaved ice from a vendor's truck fron the outdoor food court just outside the registration area, and played Stronghold until just after 10:45PM, when I returned to my hotel room, where I spent the rest of the night finishing the rest of my pizza. I didn't realize that I had forgotten to take a change of clothes with me until after I got back to my room, but I was too tired to go back down to the BYOC room to get it, so I decided I would take care of that the following day.
