Creamy's QuakeCon Photo Log

QuakeCon 2001 - Because I didn't have a camera of my own back in 2001, my father took the following pictures of the Polycounters who attended that year's QuakeCon, including myself.

QuakeCon 2003 - I apologise in advance for the poor quality of the pictures in this section. I was stuck with a Veo 1300 digital camera for my photo log for the 2003 QuakeCon. It's a good thing that I got the camera for free, because it's probably one of the shittiest cameras out there.

QuakeCon 2006 - The overall quality of these pictures is far superior than those in 2003, because this time around I brought along a Sony Cybershot DSC-P92 digital camera, instead of the piece of shit Veo 1300.

QuakeCon 2008 - I didn't take as many pictures as I did two years ago, but I still think you'll be pleased with the results.

QuakeCon 2010 - This year's QuakeCon was the first time I used my Sony Cybershot DSC-W330 digital camera, which performs better in low light than the DSC-P92 model.

QuakeCon 2012 - Once again, I'm using my Sony Cybershot DSC-W330 digital camera to take the pictures of my QuakeCon vacation.

QuakeCon 2014 - For the third QuakeCon event in a row, I take along my Sony Cybershot DSC-W330 digital camera. I was having some focusing problems while shooting some of the movies, but that didn't seem to be an issue when taking the still shots.
