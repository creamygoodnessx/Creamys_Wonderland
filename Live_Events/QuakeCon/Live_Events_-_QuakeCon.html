Creamy's QuakeCon Adventures

> Once In a while, I have the privilege of attending the QuakeCon, a giant LAN party where a bunch of guys gather in Texas to play network games for three days straight. Some of my experiences are good, some great, and some downright sucky, but in the end, I come home wanting more.

Reports - Detailed reports of my QuakeCon vacations as well as I can remember.

Photos - QuakeCon photos, most of which I took myself.

Movies - Short video clips I took of some of my experiences.

Recorded Games - Recordings of various games I played on QuakeCon's network.
