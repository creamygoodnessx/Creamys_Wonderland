News Archive:
9/1/2009 to 12/15/2010

12/15/2010 - This is probably going to be the last update of the year, so I might as well make it a big one. Version 1.3 of my Brandy Oteigh_MUGEN_character is now available for download, so you all can head on down to the Games / MUGEN section and get your grubby little hands on it now.

10/2/2010 - What, another update only four days after the last one? That's right ladies and gentlemen, that's exactly what this is. Why? Well, about one or two o'clock this morning, I realised that today is Creamy's Wonderland's eleventh birthday. Unfortunately, I don't really have any good updates to post at this time, except for putting up the next round of the Double Jeopardy 2008_tournament, which can be found in the Games / Mugen section as always. Also, I just managed to fix the front page mural's border by lowering the tables' width from 690 to 684 pixels.

9/28/2010 - And now, after a month and a half after the end of the event, I present to you the very belated report of my QuakeCon vacation of 2010, which can be found in the Live Events section of the site. Also, losers' final match A of the Double Jeopardy 2008_tournament is up on in the Games / Mugen section. 

> I understand that I promised to have the videos of the tournament on my CreamyGoodnessX_YouTube channel by now. However, I underestimated the sheer amount of work involved in creating the intro movies, and other projects took priority over the tournament, so I was unable to deliver on that promise. However, I'm going to dedicate the month of October to getting the first of the vids ready, so expect to see some remastered episodes of the tournament by the end of the month.

5/5/2010 - Version 1.2 of my Brandy Oteigh_MUGEN character has been released, and includes several updates, mainly fixes for all missing sprites and animations. Also, the Double Jeopardy 2008_tournament is drawing to a close, because the winners' final match is now viewable. The tournament is already taking up over a gigabyte of space on my Huge Laser account, so on the update after the final match of the tournament is posted, I will delete all the tournament videos from Creamy's Wonderland, and make them available for request only. I will also start posting the matches on my CreamyGoodnessX channel on YouTube sometime within the next two or three months.

3/12/2010 - The losers' semi-finals round B of the Double Jeopardy 2008_tournament is now available for the world to see. Check it out in the Games / MUGEN section, as always.

> And now, for some pretty damn big news. After over two years of on and off work, and after overcoming countless obstacles and frustrations, the initial release my Brandy Oteigh MUGEN character is finally completed! Enjoy.

3/2/2010 - In this first update of the new decade, I've done some work on the Games / MUGEN section of Creamy's Wonderland. Since the last update, I've completed seven new animations for my MUGEN character_Brandy Oteigh, including her last special attack, and her first super attack, the Oteigh Drop. Also, the losers' semi-finals round A of the Double Jeopardy 2008_tournament is now up and running.

11/29/2009 - I have two more sections of Creamy's Wonderland fully operational, and this time it's Live Events and the Art Gallery. As for the Games / MUGEN section, I've completed Brandy Oteigh's Ura Nage, as well as a third field test movie showing off her new features. I've also updated the Double Jeopardy 2008_tournament, which now includes the winners' semi-finals round.

11/1/2009 - The Builds section of Creamy's Wonderland is now completely up and running, as well as a new section called Contacts. There are also some more updates in Games / MUGEN. I finished Brandy Oteigh's Power Bomb and Stunned animations, bringing the total number of completed animations to 55 out of a posible 62. I've also redesigned her future Casual outfit to include shorts and a baseball cap. Finally, the losers' quater-finals round B of the Double Jeopardy 2008_tournament is now available for your viewing pleasure.

10/2/2009 - After the initial move to Huge Laser, I set out to recover and graphically overhaul Creamy's Wonderland in one month's time, but I underestimated the sheer size of the task and failed miserably. Right now, only the Games and Links sections are up and running, as well as parts of the Builds section. Over the next few months, I'll be restoring the rest of the site one section at a time. As for actual updates, I've completed two more animations for my Brandy Oteigh_MUGEN character, which are the low palm thrust and the knee smash.

> By the way, I didn't realize this until I started typing out this front page report, but today is the tenth aniversary of Creamy's Wonderland. I've always wanted to do some kickass mural celebrating the event, but it doesn't look like that's going to happen. :( This site had gone through many revisions and survived at least three potential shut downs, but despite it all it's still here and hanging tough. Happy birthday Creamy's Wonderland, and let's hope for many more.

9/1/2009 - Welcome to the new Creamy's Wonderland, now coming at you from Huge Laser. Unfortunately, I didn't have time to carry all of my content over from IGN before the big crunch, because a move like this involves not only copying and pasting URLs, but changing the internal links in each page, as well as switching to Google ad banners. For now, all you're going to see is this front page, but I'll have everything else up and running in the next update, with the exception of the Evil Blood section which I'm discontinuing permanently, as the new front page mural depicts. I have a graphical overhaul of the site planned out, and I think you're all going to like what I have in mind.
