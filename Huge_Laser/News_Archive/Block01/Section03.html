News Archive - Section 3

8/1/2000 - Five new weapon models for Brandy Hothiegh have been completed in the past six days. Head on over to Tier 2 of the Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics section and take a look. In addition, Tier 3 has begun.

7/27/2000 - As Tier 2 of my Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics project draws to a close, I've made a few changes to my schedule. I have decided to make the first movie of the tactical guide a lesson on config files, and I'm setting up my schedule accordingly.

7/19/2000 - After completing the Smartskin on the skirt of my Jatoten Rascot Battle Mage model, I've finally completely finished the task. It is been 16 days of some pretty tough work, and I'm now eight days behind schedule, but the important thing is that the model is behind me. 

7/15/2000 - The textures for my Jatoten Rascot Battle Mage model are in. As always, you can see it on the BJQ2T page.

7/13/2000 - All right, I am now two days behind schedule, but I finally got the mesh of my Jatoten Rascot Battle Mage model finished. The results of my work can be seen on the BJQ2T page.

7/7/2000 - I finished the head of my Jatoten Rascot Battle Mage model yesterday. This puts me only a day behind schedule on my BJQ2T project.

7/1/2000 - It's been a while since my last update, and since then, I've gotten quite a bit done on IWOW. The NIS FTP server was down for a while, preventing me from making any uploads to this site. After I complained to the good people at NIS about the problem, they managed to get me back up to speed. I was also held at bay by a homework assignment, but now that this is all behind me, I can move on with the issues of IWOW. The first tier of my BJQ2T project is over, but I finished last month behind schedule after being so far ahead a couple weeks ago. In other news, I temporarily closed down the Xiaofauxxish section of IWOW. It will remain closed until I can develop a better conlang to replace the old one.

6/14/2000 - My Quake 2 modeling tutorial opened today. So far, only the first section of it is up and running, but I will add the other sections as my schedule permits.

6/10/2000 - I've decided that today was the day to replace the graphic in the Quake 2 main page with something original. I spent three and a half hours on the new image, and I hope you're as happy with it as I am. :)

6/9/2000 - It's time now for a news double-whammy. A few days ago, I finished optimizing the Brandy Hothiegh model for my future Quake 2 tactical guide, Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics. As a demonstration of the creasing I eliminated in the model, I have provided a three second clip of Brandy dancing, the first movie file on IWOW in fact. The clip is very basic, so don't expect too much from it. In other news, I've just ordered Animation: Master 2000, V8.5, and it should arrive at my house in two or three days. Oh, and don't be surprised if you start seeing Fortunecity banners on the site. Some of the new pages will have these banners, because I have recently expanded IWOW into Fortunecity in order to take advantage of their 100 megs of bandwidth.
