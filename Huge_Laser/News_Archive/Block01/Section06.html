News Archive - Section 6

7/3/2001 - It's been over a month since IWOW's last update, but I've gotten quite a few things done since then. The one section I gave the most attention to this time around was Stardancer's character page in the Evil Blood section. I put up a couple more sketches of her, as well as suggest an alternate plot for her in her spoiler page. I've also posted Oinkillune's height and details drawing on his character page. Speaking of character pages, I came up with my own variation of a famous set of steps for cartoon animators to create animated characters, which is now available on the main Evil Blood character page.

> If you take a quick peek around the site, you'll notice that some of the ad banners have been fixed. They went AWOL several months ago, but I never could figure out exactly why. I'll fix the rest of them on IWOW's next update. In the poll on this page, I've decided to replace Pico with Stewie Griffin from the Seth MacFarlane cartoon series Family Guy. And one more thing. I'm almost ready to officially start work on the Follycount project. All I need to do is finish writing a script for one of the show's trailers and I'll be good to go.

6/2/2001 - Today, after putting my mind on overdrive for many laborous hours, I managed to fix a small issue I've been having in Stardancer's spoiler in the Evil Blood section. I've also added my first front and profile head sketches of Dust to his page. A couple days ago, I repaired the link to the Magic Sharpie test animation on King Creamy's Follycount bio.

5/24/2001 - On the 18th, right after I updated IWOW for the 17th, I began updating the info of the Evil Blood characters Oinkillune and the Plant Queen. I also put up a first draft pic of Stardancer on her info page. As for Follycount, I updated a few of the characters' profiles, as well as add a draft pic for vogue13, Soul Ripper, and The Shining.

5/17/2001 - Yep, another upgrade already. I know I said that I'd change the poll every on major update, but obviously I've gotta give it more than a day, right? I finally got around to making the spoilers for Evil Blood characters Oinkillune, Dust, and Stardancer available. Just a word of warning, though. As many of you know, spoilers are bits of information about a story that can give away the story, so don't read the spoilers if you want to wait for me to release the comic to find out what happens. I have also updated the info on Stardancer and the Plant Queen.

5/16/2001 - I've finally gotten around to doing something for IWOW that I've wanted to do for a long time. I have added a counter to the front page, but I plan to replace it with something better looking in the future. Also, I've added a new polling system where the question will change on every major site upgrade. Seeing how I haven't been upgrading the site too frequently lately, each poll will have plenty of time to rack up votes.

4/28/2001 - It's been about a month since my last update, but after you see what I've been doing for IWOW in all this time, I think you'll agree that the wait was well worth it. I have basically dismantled the Hour Of Need story, and replaced it with a new and improved storyline called Evil Blood. Since my Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics project was partially based on HON, I renovated that as well, replacing it with Follycount, a comedic parody of the Polycount community. If my current plans remain in-tact, the old BJQ2T will be part of Follycount, with its story altered to eliminate its ties with HON.

3/27/2001 - A few days ago, I finished a quick and dirty storyboard of Scene 4-3 of BJQ2T: CFS. There's also a new short movie on my alter ego's profile page for your viewing pleasure. Also, from now until further notice, I will be focusing my efforts on completely finishing Scene 4 of BJQ2T: CFS.

> I will soon be tearing down the ENTIRE Into The Fray section of IWOW, in order to make way for my new Evil Blood online comic project. This will effect the Quake 2 section of this site, because of the ties that BJQ2T shares with ITF. Severing these ties and making new ones to accommodate the new story won't be easy, and I expect it to take several months for me to finish this critical transition.

3/23/2001 - I put up a new BJQ2T Models section in the Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics section of IWOW. Within this newpage are the profiles for two new characters, Red Storm and dDuck.

3/10/2001 - After about two weeks of frustrating hard work, I've gotten the head of the sniper in Scene 2 of BJQ2T: CFS finished. That is all.

2/18/2001 - Um, okeydoke. It's been well over a month since my last upgrade to IWOW, and during this time, I've been juggling my time between IWOW, school, and TV. In all of this chaos, I somehow managed to finish Scene 4-2 of Config File Setup of Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics. I've also started Tier 9 of the BJQ2T project.
