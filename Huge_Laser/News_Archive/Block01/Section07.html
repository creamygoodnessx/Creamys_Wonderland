8/30/2002 - Here's another late update in the series, effectively ending my "one upgrade every four days" plan. But what matters is that it's here, so who the hell cares? In this fifth upgrade of the series, I have updated Oinkillune's character profile due to the information on the Deities And Magic page. I've also eliminated Oinkillune's spoiler, since the information it once contained will now be revealed at the beginning of the story, and therefore will no longer ruin the plot. I don't know when I'm going to finish the sixth and final upgrade, but I can give you an idea of what to expect. First of all, this site will no longer be known as the Infinite World Of Wonder, but something with fewer syllables. I will introduce a new character to Evil Blood, and eliminate one who longer has a clear purpose to the story. Also, the entire site will undergo a total cosmetic makeover, making it easier to navigate, and truer to the subject matters of each section. Finally, there will be a new front-page mural, one that I've been designing for many weeks, but haven't actually started drawing yet. There will be a few other changes as well, but they're too many to list here, so I guess I have no choice but to leave you all to wonder. Muahahahaha!

That's all for now, take it easy.

8/4/2002 - I LIVE!!! It's been over FIVE months since I last updated IWOW, but let's not dwell on the past, shall we? I've had so much work building up all this time, that I decided it would be best if I broke it up into several small updates. Within the next 20 days, I plan to make one additional update at four day intervals. As the first contribution of this series of updates, I have created a new sub-section of Evil Blood by the name of Deities And Magic, which explains the origins of magic and deities on Nnet Olotor, and the connection between the two. I also wrote the first sub-section of D&M, which is Mages And Clerics. I know a lot of you have had trouble navigating through this site, but by the time these updates are through, getting around IWOW should be a lot easier. I also plan on changing the look of the site, and am even considering a possible name change. That's all for now, and if all goes well, I should have IWOW updated again on the eighth.
