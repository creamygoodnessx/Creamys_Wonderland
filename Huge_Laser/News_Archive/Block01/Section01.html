News Archive - Section 1

3/29/2000 - All righty then. Its been a while since my last update. I've been spending this time creating a new Quake 2_PPM, one which I first modelled for a contest at Polycount. The model is finished, and now makes its home on Quake2 / PPMs. Speaking of the PPMs page, the Future Models section has been removed, because I'm going to be bogged down with the mind numbing work I have to do in order to get the first few chapters of Into The Fray: Hour Of Need finished by the end of the year. You can find out more about the progress I make on this daunting project as I get it done by checking out the soon to be opened Into The Fray / Internet Show page. Getting this project done is sure to be a bitch, but then again, so is life. 

2/11/2000 - Check out the all-new House Of Cantar page in the Into The Fray / Tour Guide section of IWOW. All right, I know it's been ages since I updated the Quake 2 section, but here goes. I have just entered the modelling contest of my life. Visit the Quake 2 / PPMs section for further details on this exciting event. 

1/31/2000 - Information about the etmayoos is now available in Into The Fray / Races. I also made several fixes throughout the entire Into The Fray section of IWOW, including fixing a few spelling errors, and adding new stats to the Into The Fray / Characters section.

1/12/2000 - The bios for Thunderbird and Cardiano are up and running in the Into the Fray / Characters page. 

12/30/1999 - The Characters page is now open, and it features the profiles of Jatoten Rascot and Brandy Hothiegh.

12/25/1999 - Merry Christmas, visitors! The Races page is up and running, and it already comes with two races, quinnisalzes and ambrons. Also, the Chiddetny Page under Into The Fray / Tour Guide has been updated. 

12/20/1999 - The Chiddetny Gargantuan page is only partially completed but up and running anyway. This page is located under Into The Fray / Tour Guide / Chiddetny. I finally decided to change the name of Destination: Blarr. It was supposed to be a temporary name, one I was planning to change since I started writing the story five years ago. From now on, it will be known as 'Into The Fray: Hour Of Need'.
