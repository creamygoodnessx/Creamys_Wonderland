My Quake 2 Interests

> Welcome to the Quake2 section of Creamy's Wonderland. To me, Quake 2 is not only one of the greatest video games I've ever played, but it has also been an excellent medium of art for me. I don't play the game nearly as often as I used to, but I still get in some action whenever I can. Ever since I was 19, I've been building plugin player models of my own, and skins for others' models in my spare time. Once in a blue moon, when I play Quake 2 online, I see another player use one of my models, and it feels really great that someone out there appreciates my work. :D

PPMs - Plugin player models that Quake 2 players can use as in-game avatars.

VWEPs - Visible weapons for certain PPMs.

Skins & Icons - Textures and small portraits for certain PPMs.

Modeling Tutorial - A detailed but unfinished tutorial of how to create Quake 2 PPMs.
