MUGEN AI Tournaments

> After watching a MUGEN AI tournament, and having a great time in doing so, I decided to someday record such a tourney of my own. After spending the next few months gathering characters and stages from the Web, and downloading and learning to use CamStudio, I had all the tools I needed to start recording my first tournament. However, I could never figure out how to record decent quality matches at 10 megs a minute or less. When I figure out how to do that, or how to compress good quality movies down to this size, I will record all of the matches in one day, and then release them on the site one tournament round at a time.

Double Jeopardy 2008 - A seeded double elimination tournament of 62 matches in 14 rounds of combat.
