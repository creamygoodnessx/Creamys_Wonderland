Creamy's Wonderland News Archive

News Archive:
11/24/2K to 1/22/2001

1/22/2001 - I now have a new link in the often overlooked Links section of IWOW. It is the Electronic Frontier Foundation's Blue Ribbon Campaign website, something I should have put up a long, long time ago. In lesser news, a new test animation is available in the BJQ2T main page.

1/9/2001 - Scene 7 of Config File Setup of Brandy and Jatoten's Quake2 Tactics is now behind me, and a new Update Info section has been added to the Goodies page of BJQ2T. That is all.

1/7/2001 - And now, the fist update to IWOW of the millennium. (For those of you who consider 2001 to be the start of the millennium rather than 2000, that is.) The sixth scene of  the Config File Setup episode of BJQ2T is now available in its release form. Also, I've made a slight change to the design of the sniper model.

12/28/2K - The concept sketches of the sniper from the Config File Setup episode of BJQ2T are now completed. With this task out of the way, the seventh tier of the BJQ2T project comes to an end. I'm also planning on converting the Brandy Hothiegh model into a Quake2 PPM.

12/24/2K - This site update is a rather minor one compared to the ones I've been doing recently. I've taken a few screenshots of Who Wants to be a Millionaire and Hey Arnold. I will use these screenshots as sources in order to help me draw a concept sketch of the sniper from Scene 2 of the Brandy and Jatoten's Quake2 Tactics episode Config File Setup.

12/20/2K - I've been falling behind in my news bearing responsibilities lately. Since my last site update eleven days ago, a lot's been going on with my BJQ2T project. The deadline for BJQ2T as my 2D Computer Animation project was yesterday, and as a result of my underestimation of the project, the only thing I was able to hand in was the first four scenes, the first three being their release versions, and the fourth was only the draft version of the first part. The rest of the news is too much to list here on the main page, but you can read all about it on the BJQ2T page.

12/9/2K - Scene 7 of no-frills CFS of BJQ2T is now finished, which means that all seven scenes of the episode are now at least in the completed draft state. I also finished synchronizing the video and audio tracks of the first three scenes, promoting them to release level.

12/1/2K - Tier 7 of my BJQ2T project has begun. I had to move back the deadline of this tier to the 19th, because that's when it's due as my 2D Computer Animation 2 homework project.

11/29/2K - The Quake2/BJQ2T section Infinite World of Wonder now has a new section, the BJQ2T news archive. You can check that out, as well as further progress on the BJQ2T project, on the BJQ2T page.

11/24/2K - Scene 6 of the no-frills version of the Config File Setup episode of Brandy and Jatoten's Quake2 Tactics is now ready for the eyes of the public. I've also began recording the voice overs for the first three scenes of BJQ2T. The new picture you see above shows the new Polycount IWOW web addy.
