Stardancer
(10/20/2002 to 6/29/2005)

 - CHARACTER STATS -
Real Name - Can't remember
Race - Human
Residence - Can't remember
Demeanor - Meek, Squeamish
Age - Approximately 13
Height/Weight - 5' 7" / 115lbs
Alignment - Neutral Good

> Stardancer is a young human teenage girl, approximately 13 years of age. She is the classic peaches and cream type; shy and reserved, passive and soft spoken. She is good natured, polite and 'lady-like', but is extremely pessimistic, and often lacks the confidence to express her thoughts. When Stardancer enters the story, she is found naked and unconscious on a beach by our heroes. Since there is no wreckage found near Stardancer, the heroes conclude that she fell overboard from a passing vessel and then washed ashore. She has no memory of her past, and can't remember her family, where she lives, or even her name. As an improvisation, one of our heroes gives her a temporary name, which will be 'Stardancer' until I can come up with something more relevant. (Whoever gives the girl her name will most likely choose a name that's dear to him/her.) Since the party is headed for the city which bears the Orb of Knowledge, Jonny-V suggests that Stardancer pay a visit to the Orb in the hopes that doing so will help Stardancer regain her memory, and reunite her with her loved ones.

> Our heroes had no trouble gaining Stardancer's trust, and they quickly formed a strong emotional bond with her, especially Oinkillune who began seeing her as an ideal sister. Stardancer has a strong dislike against weapons and other things that kill. She is uncomfortable being near weapons of any kind, even those in the hands of her new found friends. When her life is threatened, Stardancer's reaction is to beg for mercy or hide behind her friends, rather than to defend herself. However, as the story progresses, she slowly adjusts to her temporary but dangerous lifestyle, and is only beginning to understand the party's need to defend itself.
