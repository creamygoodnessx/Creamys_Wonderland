Binky
(4/28/2001 to 10/2/2002)

 - CHARACTER STATS -
Real Name - Taelin
Race - Monkey-like humanoid (yet to be named)
Residence - ?
Demeanor - Reckless, Hyperactive, Giggly
Age - 9
Height/Weight - 3' 2" / 41lbs
Alignment - Chaotic Good

> Binky is Oinkillune's nine year-old little sister. Although her name is Taelin, she prefers to call herself 'Binky' for short. (Remember, names are subject to change.) She is far too brave for her own good, frequently getting into all kinds of trouble with the authorities, mainly for stealing, trespassing, and picking other people's  locks. Binky has a natural flair for adventure, and often sneaks out of town to go exploring in the wilderness, frequently getting into life-threatening situations. In such cases, her adopted brother Oinkillune has to find her, rescue her if necessary, and then escort her home. Oinkillune has always been Binky's sworn protector, but during their adventures together since they left home for the Orb, Binky is convinced that it's the other way around.

> Because Binky's parents are locksmiths, Binky had the chance to become an extraordinary thief at an early age. Whenever she was caught stealing, or doing some other mischievous action, her parents punished her by locking her in the supply room, which held locks of various types, keys, and a few other items. At first, Binky would pass the time by seeing which keys fit which locks. When that lost it's fun, Binky began fiddling with the locks by jamming a variety of objects into them. When Binky was sent to the supply room the first few times, her lock picking attempts resulted in disappointment. Eventually, she began meeting with success. Her lock picking skills improved steadily, so that by the time she was eight, she had the lock picking skills of an experienced thief, and could pick a variety of locks with relative ease. Binky also developed a skill in escaping from several types of traps and binds. She would occasionally perform feats of escape in the village's equivalent of a town square. One day, when Binky was performing a particularly dangerous stunt, something went wrong and Binky was nearly killed. At least, she would have been if Oinkillune hadn't intervened. (I still haven't decided on the exact stunt she attempted.) As shaken up as Binky was about the near-tragedy, her dreams of becoming a world-famous escape artist remained rock-solid.
