Age - 15
Height/Weight - 5' 1" / 105lbs
Alignment - Lawful Good

> Oinkillune is a young elf who was adopted by a monkey-like race of forest humanoids, and lives in their village in the trees. Although he's the only elf in the village, and although he isn't naturally equipped to live high in the trees (not having a tail or hands for feet), he is rarely picked on. He not only has a magnetic personality, but also a good heart, and always puts the needs of others over his own. Oinkillune's greatest challenge in his life is taking care of his little sister, Binky, and keeping her out of trouble.Their parents are long-time locksmiths, and while this influenced Binky to become a thief, Oinkillune chose pursue his interest in the sword and dreamed of someday becoming a knight (or some similar authority figure who defends the weak and downtrodden from evil). Oinkillune is about 15, and he has recently started having strange dreams, in which the evil deity Zod pleaded with him to 'free' him, and promising the young elf great power if he did. Oinkillune thinks he might be going insane, and tells his family about the dreams. They insist that he visit the Orb of Knowledge in order to get some answers.
