Stardancer
(4/28/2001 to 10/20/2002)

 - CHARACTER STATS -
Real Name - Unknown
Race - Human
Residence - ?
Demeanor - Meek, Squeamish
Age - Approximately 13
Height/Weight - 5' 7" / 115lbs
Alignment - Neutral Good

> Stardancer is the typical 'damsel in distress' type of character. She is meek, soft-spoken, timid, and very uncomfortable in battle, or in any kind of hostile situation. When in such a predicament, she often chooses to rely on others to protect her, rather that deal with the danger herself, no matter how slight the danger may be. Stardancer enters the story naked and unconscious on the beach, an apparent victim of a shipwreck. Oinkillune, Binky, and Dust discover her, and nurse her back to health. She can't remember anything about her past, her family, or even her name. As an improvisation, Dust gives the girl the name 'Stardancer', most likely after either a pet or a song. However, Stardancer's name is, like everything else, is... yep, you guessed it, subject to change. Since Oinkillune and his party are already on their way to The Orb of Knowledge, he suggests that Stardancer join his party, and that perhaps the Orb can help her discover her identity, so she can be reunited with her loved ones.
