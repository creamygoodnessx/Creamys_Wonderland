Characters

OINKILLUNE- The underdog. The unlikely and ethically strong hero with a dark secret.

BINKY- Oinkillune's hyper, annoying, over-adventurous little sister.

JONNY-V- Emporer Stevens' runaway daughter who vows revenge on her father. Young but powerful mage.

STARDANCER- The meek and squeamish damsel in distress. Able to read other people's emotions.

PEKAIOH- Decendant of an anceint hero. Cares more about being seen as a hero than actually trying to be one.

PERNAHKO - Wise and learned bartender with a knack for solving mysteries. Fallen cleric rebuilding his reputation.

BAX'KARRA - Unwilling member of a ruthless Zodian family gang who longs to live an honest life.

KAIRAYLAH - Ancient ultra-powerful living weapon of last resort. Considerred obsolete by creators and imprisonned for eternity.

MARTIN HAMMILTON - Leiutennant in the Stevens Imperial Army, torn between his sworn duty and his conscience.

KAILAYNAH - Emporer Steven's personal elven slave, and the only person who Jonny-V ever considered a friend.

EMPEROR STEVENS - Seemingly unstoppable conquerer of Nnet Olotor. Completely bereft of any conscience.
