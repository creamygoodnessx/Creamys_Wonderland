> Hey, I'm Jason "Creamy Goodness" Tenn, formerly known as Jatoten Rascot. Welcome to my humble digital abode, Creamy's Wonderland, formerly known as the Infinite World of Wonder. This site is where I display both my 2D and 3D art, as well as my developing fantasy story, Evil Blood. This site is always being updated, but I try to work on it at a comfortable pace. Enjoy your stay.
