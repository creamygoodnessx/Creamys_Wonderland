Gift Art

> Once in a blue moon, an online buddy of mine offers to draw their own rendition of one of my characters. So far, everyone who has done so has had drawing skills far surpassing mine, which is not all that surprising, since I haven't yet perfected my skills. This page follows Creamy's Wonderland's house rule that large images will be down-sampled so that the larger dimension equals 500 pixels. However, I may make certain allowances if this reduction in size causes the image to lose a significant amount of quality.


CHARACTER(S): Oinkillune
ARTIST: Nathan 'Ezzat' Woodhead
DATE POSTED: 10-10-2002
DIMENSIONS: 414 x 500
COMMENTS: This pic of Oinkillune was e-mailed to me by an online buddy of mine I met at Cartoon and Illustration Paradise. I can't remember when Nathan first sent me this pic; the LoveLetter virus_Candybox contracted back in April deleted all of my jpgs along with their file info.



CHARACTER(S): Oinkillune
ARTIST: Nutsy
DATE POSTED: 10-10-2002
DIMENSIONS: 600 x 455
COMMENTS: This is the first rendition of Oinkillune by an artist other than myself, drawn back in April of this year. The advice given to me on that thread helped to greatly improve my anatomy drawing skills. I exempted this pic from CW's 500 pixel maximum, because the reduction took away too much quality from the image than I was comfortable with. Besides, I didn't think a 100 pixel difference would make too much of a difference anyway.
