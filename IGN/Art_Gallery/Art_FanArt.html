Fan Art

> I watch my share of TV shows, primarily animated ones on Nickelodeon, Cartoon Network, and the Disney Channel. Sometimes, I imagine the characters from these shows in situations they would never normally be in, and this inspires me to translate what's in my head into artwork that I can share with the world. The pics in this section are between 500 and 1000 pixels in either axis, and each axis is always a multiple of 50 pixels.

NAME: Keoni And Wendy
DATE POSTED: 12-9-2007
DIMENSIONS: 500 x 700
COMMENTS: During the first season of Disney's Lilo And Stitch, Lilo's boyfriend Keoni Jameson had a crush on Lilo's "Aunt" Wendy Pleakley before finding out that Pleakley was not a woman. This pic is my depiction of Keoni and Pleakley as boyfriend and "girlfriend".
