Songpacks

> Besides the official Dance Dance Revolution mixes, several DDR fans have made original mixes of their own, most of which can be found online.


CreamyMix 1 - This is my first songpack for Stepmania, and it's really been a bitch of a task so far. Certain songs had unsteady BPMs, so that I had to change the BPMs in the stepfile to keep the steps from going out of synch with the music. In the case of Breaking the Law, the BPMs in the song were so unsteady that there was a part of the song where I had to adjust the steps' BPMs on seven consecutive measures. Another problem was that I would be forced to delete certain songs after discovering minor pops and clicks in them that I never noticed before. I was in the middle of doing steps for Bad Case of Loving You, and had already cut the song down to less than two minutes when an anomaly in the mp3 forced me to abandon the song. The pack will ultimately have 20 songs, two of which I will compose myself, and two will be different versions of the same song.

OTHER PEOPLE'S SONGPACKS


PlagueMix Series - A songpack of 17 songs made arranged by PlagueFox.


Dancing WipEout - This songpack has a whopping 50 songs, and was created by Dendayne "W1p3out" Kévin.


Dancing WipEout 2 - This is Dendayne "W1p3out" Kévin's second songpack, which is still under construction.

DJ Naux Originals - Taren Nauxen's mix with nearly 100 songs. Some songs were barely audible, but I was able to boost their volume with Audacity.

Nifty DWIs - My personal favorite Stepmania mix. Features some video game remixes and a few 80s songs.

FoonMix - A very professional looking mix from Japan. Each simfile has a background movie, making the mix's smzip file exceptionally large.
