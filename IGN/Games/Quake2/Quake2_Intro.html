My Quake 2 Interests

> Welcome to the Quake2 section of Creamy's Wonderland. To me, Quake2 is not only one of the greatest video games I've ever played, but it has also been an excellent medium of art for me. I don't play the game nearly as often as I used to, but I still get in some action whenever I can. Ever since I was 19, I've been building plugin player models of my own, and skins for others' models in my spare time. Once in a blue moon, when I play Quake2 online, I see another player use one of my models, and it feels really great that someone out there appreciates my work. :D
