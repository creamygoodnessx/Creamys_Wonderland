    DESCRIPTION - Dib can be thought of as a pre-teen Fox Moulder from Jhonen Vasquez's Invader Zim. He has an obsession for the paranormal, and is a member of a secret club of fellow paranormal investigators called the the Swollen Eyeball. He has no friends whatsoever, and noone takes him or his work seriously, not even his father, Professor Membrane, or his sister, Gaz. Dib is the mortal enemy of Zim, a hostile alien of the Irken race, who has come to Earth to enslave its people, and is disguised as a human boy. Dib is the only person to know Zim's true identity and his mission, but is unable to convince anyone else of the truth.
