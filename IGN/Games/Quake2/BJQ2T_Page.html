10/2/2002 - I have decided to abort the BJQ2T project, and focus on developing the Follycount series instead. However, BJQ2T was not a total waste, as there is a lot from this project I can salvage for use Follycount. I've re-aranged this page so that it focuses on the Config File Setup episode of BJQ2T, rather than remaining open for any future development. I don't know how this page is going to turn out in the future, but it's a safe bet that this will not be its final overhaul.

4/27/2001 - If you haven't noticed by now, Brandy and Jatoten's Quake2 Tactics is now a smaller part of my new Follycount project. As a result of the fall of my Hour of Need project, BJQ2T will now take place in virtual arenas, rather than the Chiddetny Gargantuan. I just didn't want any of you thinking that BJQ2T was dead or nothin'! ;)
