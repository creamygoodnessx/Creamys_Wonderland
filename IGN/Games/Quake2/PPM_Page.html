Creamy Goodness' Quake2 Plugin Player Models

Quake 2 PPMs

> I became interested in the art of modeling Quake 2 plugin player models (PPMs) when I first visited the Q2PMP in the middle of 1998. From that moment, I became a PPM junkie, collecting good, high quality models wherever I could find them. I always wanted to get into PPM modeling myself, and had a few ideas from a few of my interests in other genres, but lacked the tools and know-how to do so. It was the middle of the July of 1999 when I got my first real break. After almost a year of searching for the right tutorials and software, I was finally ready to start my first model, Chuckie Finster.

7/1/2007 - This will be the final update that I will post on the Quake2 PPM main page. I've noticed that whenever I make an update to one of my models, I report it on up to three different pages on the site, and I'm beginning to think that that's a bit excessive. Model updates are reported on the front page, and in the build log of the model I'm updating if the model has one, making reporting on this page redundant and unecessary. Therefore, I've decided to stop posting updates on this page, and to let all of the page's updates, including this one, fall into the Quake2 Archive when their turns come.

10/22/2004 - Over the past couple weeks, I've been working on the beautification of the Quake 2 section of Creamy's Wonderland. As the Dib model was nearing completion, I thought it would be nice to make the next site update a little more special by not only including the new Dib model, but also by improving the appearance of the site. The changes I made consist mainly of changing the mural on the Quake 2 section's front page, replacing the tiny icons for the Quake 2 PPMs with large, trading card-like icons, and completely overhauling the PPM pages. I also changed the font of the Quake 2 pages to one called SpaceOne, which you can get here. You can also get this copy of the font I have saved locally on my PlanetQuake server.

3/20/2003 - From this point forward, I will post all Quake PPM works in progress on this page, so that you can see the progress I'm making with each one. There are two models for Quake 2 I'm currently working on. Creamy Goodness, formerly known as Jatoten Rascot Battle Mage, is a model I completed quite a while back, but only now am I finding the time to put it into a game environment. If the updates on the model's page look familiar, that's because CG was part of two projects I was doing a while back. After I finish CG as a Quake model, I probably won't do any more major work on it.

> Over a year and a half ago, I did a poll on the front page of Creamy's Wonderland, asking what cartoon character you would like to see as a Quake 2 or Quake 3 model. The winner of that poll was Dib, and I'm sure that most people who visit this site regularly (all 12 of you) were starting to doubt if I'd ever get around to getting Dib into the arenas. Well, as it turns out, I finally had the chance to do just that, and you can now see the model as it gets built step by step.
