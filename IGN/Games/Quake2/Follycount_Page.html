Follycount:
The Quake Parody Internet Series

> Welcome to Follycount, my personal parody of the Polycount community and the Quake trilogy in general. Follycount will be an animated Internet series that will depict the Internet as a vast virtual reality world, where people get sucked into their monitors when they go online, and where plugin player models come to life upon creation and are self-aware. The story focuses around six clans, each resembling its own nation within the VR Internet, and each with its own virtual reality chat room and game servers. Follycount will consist of short episodes of various types, some of them chat room conversations on various topics, such as rocket vs. rail, dealing with campers, and what the Quake3 gladiators do in their spare time. There will also be a few trailers, such as a Capture the Flag parody of the opening scene of Saving Private Ryan, and parody of a Pentium III commercial, where three people find creative ways to paint a giant Quake2 symbol on the floor with blood. And last but not least, I'll try to bring back BJQ2T in a new and improved form.

10/2/2002 - It may be hard to believe, but it's been exactly one year since I paid any attention to this section of my site. During this time, I've been focusing on the development of Evil Blood, as well as taking care of my academic needs at Bergen. My primary problem with developing Follycount is that I've never been satisfied with its characters. It's not easy for me to achieve a cast with a balance of personalities of people I know in real life, and stereotypical characters on the Internet, not to mention deciding what to do with what's left of Brandy and Jatoten. I already have a few story ideas in mind, but without a cast of characters, there's just not much I can do with them. That's why I've decided to officially suspend the Follycount project, at least until I can come up with some decent characters. I've also taken down the characters page, and it will remain unavailable until the cast is fully re-assembled. When I eventually do get Follycount up and running again, I'll make damn sure it was well worth the wait. Trust me.

10/2/2001 - There have been some critical changes to the Follycount series. Rather than have the series revolve around a single virtual chat room, I've decided to follow a theme that resembles my old BJQ2T project. Now, Follycount will be just one of six Quake clans the story of Follycount covers, and some of the characters Clan Follycount have been relocated to these other clans. I'm also bringing back a few of my old characters from the old BJQ2T. In addition, I've made a few changes to the descriptions of some of the characters, and eliminated a few characters as well. As for the minor changes, I've fixed a few wayward links in the BJQ2T section.
