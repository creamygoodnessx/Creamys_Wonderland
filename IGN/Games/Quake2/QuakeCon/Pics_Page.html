QuakeCon 2006 - The overall quality of these pictures is far superior than those in 2003, because this time around I brought along a Sony Cybershot digital camera, instead of the peice of shit Veo 1300.

QuakeCon 2008 - I didn't take as many pictures as I did two years ago, but I still think you'll be pleased with the results.
