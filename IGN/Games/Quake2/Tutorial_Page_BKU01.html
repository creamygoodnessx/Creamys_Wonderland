My Quake2 Modeling Tutorial

> Finally, after over eight months since I planned to go through with this page during IWOW's grand opening, my modeling tutorial is showing it's first signs of life. This tutorial will take you through the procedure I used to create my Chuckie Finster and Tony Toponi PPMs. Be forewarned that this tutorial does not go into great detail of how any particular software works, and is not a substitute for an instruction manual of any program mentioned within. Instead, this tutorial is for people who are proficient in the mentioned programs, and simply shows one way of turning an Animation: Master model into a fully operational PPM. It also gives advice on certain tricks you may not already be familiar with. Hopefully, this tutorial will be as easy to follow as I hope it is.

WHAT YOU NEED

- Animation: Master '99 or later
- 3dsMax export plugin
- gmax
- Quake2 Modeler
- Npherno's Skin Tool
- Paintshop Pro
