Follycount - Update Information

> Here's how the Follycount movies are updated and classified on IWOW. First, I create a quick and dirty preview of what to expect, in the form of a draft version movie. As long as a scene is in it's draft stage, it will be classified as Version 0.x. The primary functions of the draft movies are to create a foundation on which to build the release versions of the Follycount episodes on, and to give the public a quick and dirty preview of things to come. The two most important features of any draft movie in this project are proper timing and ease of upgrading. Draft scenes are compressed in the medium quality, super-compressed DivX Fast Motion format.

> A scene is officially dubbed 'release' when the crucial parts are included, and when the voice track and other necessary sounds are applied to the scene. Release versions are classified as Version 1.x and higher, and are preceded by a five second IWOW splash screen. (I plan to improve on this screen at some later date.) After that, upgrades to the scene include smoothing out the camera movement, 'humanizing' the animations, adding in backgrounds, adding in the extra models, and a few other improvements as well. Like the draft versions, release versions will also be compressed in Fast Motion. All draft and release movie files that are in excess of 10 megs in file size will be zipped. If any zip files exceed 10 megs, sound quality will be sacrificed in order to bring down the file size.

> After all conceivable upgrades have been made to a scene, it is dubbed 'final' and compressed in the higher quality Low Motion DivX format. After which, only previously unforeseen critical errors can persuade me to make further upgrades to the scene, at which time lesser changes can also be made. Only scenes in their final versions may exceed 10 megs.
