* Brandy Hothiegh's VWEP *

    DEADLINE: 8/31/2K - Brandy Hothiegh's VWEP is the first one I've ever attempted in which the character holds the weapon with both hands. Considering this, it's a damn good thing I've learned how to use some of AM2000's constraints. Brandy's super-shotgun, chaingun, and BFG are the only weapons that remain to be finished.

8/25/2K - And now, after over a month of working on this damn VWEP, it finally done. Here are Brandy's three final weapons, starting with the earliest.

> The first of the three weapons mentioned above is Brandy's all-powerful BFG, completed on the 21st of this month. Its design was inspired by the weapons of the Real Ghostbusters.

> Next we have Brandy's super shotgun, which I modeled after Ash's Boomstick from Army of Darkness. (the 12-guage double-barreled Remmington) This sweet baby was finished on the 23rd. It's got a walnut stock, cobalt blue steel, and a really groovy muzzle flash. YOU GOT THAT!?

> Brandy's next and final weapon completed was the chaingun, which I  finished last night. (Its completion was officially the 24th, since I finished a few seconds before the stroke of midnight. I made further adjustments to the chaingun this morning.) I was going to go for a ZF-1 type of weapon from the 5th Element, but the way Brandy holds her weapons forced me to alter its design.

> Well, I guess this concludes Tier 3. I'm finishing six days early, and I've decided to put those days into extra time for the next tier. Bring on Tier 4!

8/1/2K - All righty then. I have completed five of Brandy's weapon models since the last update to IWOW, and here they are in order of their completion, starting with the earliest.

> On the 28th of this month, I finished the grenade launcher, and the ballflinger which will be used as the hand grenades model.

> Yesterday, I found a replacement for Brandy's sniper rifle which she was to use as her railgun model. This enchanted crossbow fits her perfectly, IMHO.

> And finally, Brandy's blaster and rocket launcher were finished today. Expect to see an update for the rocket launcher sometime in the near future.

> I couldn't finish Brandy's entire VWEP within Tier 2, but with only three of its weapons remaining, finishing it in Tier 3 on time should be a piece of cake.

7/27/2K - Yesterday, I finished the meshes for Brandy Hothiegh's machine gun and hyperblaster weapon models, bringing her total of weapons so far to four. I plan to do two more tonight, so I guess I'm on some kind of roll now. :D
