Config List

> Here is a list of many settings, controls, and aliases supported by Quake2. Anything I don't know is either marked with a question mark, or not listed at all.

SETTINGS

cl_pitchspeed - ?
cl_run - This is your 'Always Run' setting. It causes your character to run without holding down the run key. When this setting is on, the run key causes your character to walk. 0 is off, 1 is on.
cl_maxfps - Maximum frames per second. The optimal setting for dial-up connections or slower machines is 30. Anything higher may cause extra lag, unless you have a more powerful system and a faster connection. The higher this setting is set, the smoother the game will run, but the higher the setting, the more demanding it is on your system.
crosshair - The tiny crosshair you see at the center of your screen during gameplay. 1 is cross, 2 is dot, 3 is angle, 0 is no crosshair.
freelook - Allows you to aim with your mouse, so that your crosshair follows your mouse's movements. 0 is off, 1 is on. THIS SHOULD ALWAYS BE ON!
hand - This is your Handedness setting. It determines what hand you see your character firing with. 0 is ?, 1 is ?, 2 is ?.
m_filter - ?
m_pitch - ?
sensitivity - This is your Mouse Speed setting. Higher settings will increase the sensitivity or you mouse. I recommend a setting of 15.
fov - Field of View. The higher the number, the further your camera is zoomed out. This setting must be 90 or less if you want to see your character holding your selected weapon. Setting fov too high or too low, obscures vision and make the game unplayable. I recommend a setting of between 120 and 180.
skill - The difficulty setting of the single-player game. 1 is easy, 2 is medium, 3 is hard.
gender - Your character's gender. This setting will only effect the messages in the console, and won't effect gameplay in any way. 0 is neutral, 1 is male, 2 is female.
spectator - Spectator mode. Most servers that run Quake2 version 3.19 or higher support this. 0 is off, 1 is on.

CONTROLS

+attack - Attack
+forward - Walk forward
+back - Backpedal
+left - Turn left
+right - Turn right
? - Run
+moveleft - Strafe left
+moveright - Strafe right
? - Sidestep
+lookup - Look up
+lookdown - Look down
centerview - Center view
mlook - Mouse look
klook - Keyboard look
+moveup - Jump
+movedown - Crouch
invuse - Use item
invdrop - Drop item
invprev - Previous item
invnext - Next item
cmd help - Help computer

(A + symbol means the control will remain active only as long as the key it's bound to remains held down.)

ALIASES

Sniper Zoom
alias +sniperzoom "fov 20;m_pitch 0.004;m_yaw 0.004"
alias -sniperzoom "fov 120;m_pitch 0.022;m_yaw 0.022"

Grappling Hook
alias +grapplinghook "hook"
alias -grapplinghook "unhook"

(In order to bind an alias to a key, simply include the line bind (key) "+(alias)". For example, bind e "+grapplinghook" binds the e key to the grappling hook. Include the + if the control should only be active as long as the key is held down.)
