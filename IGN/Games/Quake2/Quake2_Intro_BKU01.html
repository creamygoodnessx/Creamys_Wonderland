My Quake 2 Interests

> Welcome to the Quake2 section of Creamy's Wonderland. To me, Quake 2 is not only the greatest video game I've ever played, but it has also been an excellent medium of art for me. Not only do I play the game at least a few times a week, I've also taken interest in designing custom skins for different PPMs created by third parties. I've also gotten around to completing a PPM of my own. So far, however, I haven't seen anyone use my PPM or skins while playing online :(.
