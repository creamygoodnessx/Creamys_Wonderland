The Creamy Entertainment System

> Ever since I attended my first QuakeCon, I was fascinated by the different designs of the case mods some of the attendees brought in. I thought about building my own computer, but didn't actually start one until last November. My first custom-built computer is a compact design, built after the many gaming consoles available. I call my build the Creamy Entertainment System, a console-like name with my personal touch.
