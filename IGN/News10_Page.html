News Archive:
10/2/2004 to 7/1/2007

7/1/2007 - After another super-long hiatus, this one for nine months, I finally found time to update Creamy's Wonderland. I don't have any new works to offer this time around, but I did update a lot of my existing works in the Games pages. In the Quake2 section, the Tony Toponi PPM has a new rocket launcher model, which looks more like a genuine Chinese firecracker. The Dib PPM now has a permanent fist for a right hand, which fixes some of the models's errors and allows Dib to have a full VWEP that doesn't distort too badly. As for the Stepmania section, I updated all of the songs in CreamyMix 1, and most of these updates involve changing the steps to make them easier to play on a dance pad. Finally, I've posted Section 15 of CES's written log in the Builds section.

10/2/2006 - Today, Creamy's Wonderland turns seven, but because I didn't remember my site's birthday until the last minute, I didn't have time to set up the usual CES Log update. In addition to this birthday announcement, I've made a link to Machinae Supremacy's official site on the Links page.

8/30/2006 - In today's site update, I have the report for my QuakeCon 2006 vacation in the Quake2 section of Creamy's Wonderland. I also have Section 14 of CES's written log up and running.

7/1/2006 - As you may have noticed over the past few months, many links throughout Creamy's Wonderland were broken. This is because all GameSpy cottages were moved to a new server, and because I renamed several images and folders to names I felt were neater and easier to remember. However, this update should fix most if not all of the broken links. In addition, I've updated most of the songs in CreamyMix 1, and these updates consist mainly of resynchs. And as in the case of all recent site updates, I have added another update page to the CES build log, this time Section 13.

2/18/2006 - This is the first site update of the year, and I have a pretty decent amount of work to show for it. Five more stepfiles have been added to my Stepmania mix, CreamyMix 1. I also made various updates to the characters in Evil Blood story, and added a new magic technique to the Magic Techniques sub-section of Deities & Magic. And finally, as with every recent update, I put up a new CES build log page (Section 12), that adds the usual ten new dates to the log.

10/2/2005 - There has been a major change to the directory structure of Creamy's Wonderland. I put up a new Games section to the site, and put the Quake2 section under it. Joining Quake2 is a new sub-section under Games called Stepmania, which is where I will post CreamyMix 1, a pack of twenty stepfiles I'm currently working on. In the future, there might be more songpacks, dancers, and even themes, if I ever learn how to make them.

> Finally, as with every update nowadays, I've added another section to CES's written log, and this time it's Section 11.

9/5/2005 - Despite being caught up in several important tasks, such as job hunting and finishing my CES project, I managed to get some updates done on Creamy's Wonderland over the past two months. In the Evil Blood section, I made an update to Binky's character bio, added a new Magic Techniques sub-section to Deities & Magic, and applied the Tempus Sans font to the entire Evil Blood section. Speaking of fonts, I also applied a Comic Sans font to the Art Gallery section. I also made a long overdue update to the Links page. Finally, I added another ten-upgrade section to CES's written log; Section 10.

6/29/2005 - It's been over eight months since my last update, but I couldn't help that, being busy working on building my compact PC, the Creamy Entertainment System, and preparing for a big move to south Jersey. As a matter of fact, the house is almost completely bare and full of boxes as I type this, and my folks are moving down to our new house tomorrow. Anyways, as far as Creamy's Wonderland goes, I did some work on the Evil Blood/Characters section, updating Stardancer's bio, and introducing a new character; Pekaioh. I also added another ten-update page (Section 9) to CES's written log. A few months ago, my brother-in-law JD visited CES's photo log, and told me that the pictures took a long time to load. So I've since implemented real thumbnails to the photo log, instead of having the full pictures shrunken down to 100 x 100 pixels like I did before.

10/22/2004 - The first official version of my Dib_Quake2 PPM (Version 1.1) has just been released. It still has plenty of flaws, but I plan to fix them for the Version 2.1 release. Speaking of PPMs, I finally decided it was time to give up on my Creamy Goodness model, and discontinue work on it indefinitely. Also, I overhauled the entire Quake2 section of Creamy's Wonderland, giving all of it's pages the SpaceOne font. I paid particular attention to Quake2's PPMs sub-section, giving it an entire new look. In the Builds section, I have uploaded the next ten updates (Section 8) to my CES build log, and I will release ten more every time I update the site. Oh by the way, I also made the font of the Builds icon a little bigger, to match the font size of the Links icon.

As you can see, I have a new front page mural commemorating the site's fifth birthday. It's something I whipped up at the last minute, but I think it turned out pretty damn good for a rush job. In Evil Blood section, Oinkillune's and Jonny-V's bios have been updated. As for the Quake2 section, I finished the third section of my Quake2 modeling tutorial: Optimization. I also fixed a few links on the tutorial's main page, as well as make locally stored copies of modeling tools available in the event that their original locations are down.
