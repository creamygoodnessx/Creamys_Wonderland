MUGEN Lifebars

> Several years after initially getting into MUGEN, and after creating my first character and working on a second, I finally began experimenting with lifebars. For those who don't know what lifebars are, they are the indicators that show how much health and energy each fighter has, and also shows how much time remains in the current round, amongst other things.


Radio Lifebar - Based on the LCD screen of your typical car radio, the Radio Lifebar gives the feel of a back lit, colorful digital display.
