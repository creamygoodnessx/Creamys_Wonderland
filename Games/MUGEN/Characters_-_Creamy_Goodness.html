Story
> Creamy Goodness is the mayor of the Internet city of Creamy's Wonderland, and has been in charge of maintaining it for over a decade. Although he has an eccentric and playful, sometimes even seemingly childish and immature exterior, his tireless and thorough maintenance of the city shows a more mature side to him that surfaces when necessary. Creamy is a reclusive and private individual, spending most of his time locked in his bedroom/office, and rarely interacts with other people outside of the few communities he attends for entertainment. These communities include a martial arts club, a clan of FPS gladiators, as well as a few others.

> Creamy's story is not very well developed as of this date, but at some point in his backstory, he gains the power to create green orbs of magical energy in his hands. Exactly how he comes across this power is currently a mystery. Perhaps it was a prize for winning some kind of contest, or a gift from someone as a reward for saving that person's life. Maybe someone lost possession of the source of the power, and then Creamy came across it by chance. However Creamy gains this power, he currently uses it to supplement his martial arts abilities, and has integrated it into his fighting style. With the vastly increased potential the power has given him, Creamy decides to enter the next Netizen Rage martial arts tournament and put his fighting skills to the ultimate test.
