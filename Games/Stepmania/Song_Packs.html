Song Packs

> Besides the official Dance Dance Revolution mixes, several DDR fans have made original mixes of their own, most of which can be found online.


CreamyMix 1 - This is my first song pack for Stepmania, and it's really been a bitch of a task so far. Certain songs had unsteady BPMs, so that I had to change the BPMs in the stepfile to keep the steps from going out of synch with the music. In the case of Breaking the Law, the BPMs in the song were so unsteady that there was a part of the song where I had to adjust the steps' BPMs on seven consecutive measures. Another problem was that I would be forced to delete certain songs after discovering minor pops and clicks in them that I never noticed before. I was in the middle of doing steps for Bad Case of Loving You, and had already cut the song down to less than two minutes when an anomaly in the mp3 forced me to abandon the song. The pack will ultimately have 20 songs, two of which I will compose myself, and two will be different versions of the same song.
