DESCRIPTION - Austavin is the Internet persona of my oldest nephew Austin Luke. Up until recently, I used to tease him a lot, as shown in my Teasing Austin series. However, in July of 2015, I issued a challenge to Austin where I would show him something that was meant to anger him, and if he got through the whole thing without getting upset, I would keep my teasing of him to a minimum. I would also make the creation of a Quake 2 plugin player model of him, which had already been underway since May of that year, a priority. I showed him a mock Stepmania mix where I had collected existing songs and changed their names to ones that were meant to upset him, such as "I Said NOOOOO!!!!!", and "I'm A Pretty Boy". Surprisingly, Austin won the challenge, putting the creation of his PPM on the fast track to completion.
