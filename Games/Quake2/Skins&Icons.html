4/25/2013 - Just when you thought there would never be another update to the Skins & Icons page, THIRTEEN FUCKING YEARS LATER, I come up with this little gem. I have never been satisfied with the icon for the Black Cow skin, so yesterday, I went into Adobe Photoshop and created a new icon. The Creamy Icon Pack has been updated with the two new skins, and you can get the new skin pack here.

> While playing in Quake 2 deathmatches online a few years ago, I would occasionally see a player by the name of big_g, and I'd notice that he would use the Cartman PPM with a skin named "black". I didn't have the skin he was using, so I saw big_g as a grunt. I did however have the model, so I could hear him as Cartman. Since then, I was searching the Web in vain for this mysterious Black skin. Finally, I was playing against big_g one day, and at the start of one of the rounds, the server uploaded the skin to me, much to my surprise. However, it was the Black skin for the wrong model; Brian Yee's Eric_C model, and this apparantly caused Quake 2 to crash. I wanted to keep playing against big_g, but wanted to tell him apart from the other players. So I created a Black skin of my own for the Cartman model. The skin didn't come out as well as I would have liked it to, but I've never been that much of a skinner. The Creamy Skin Pack now includes the new Black skin, and you can get the skin pack here.
