News Archive - Section 4

11/1/2000 - Tier 5 of the BJQ2T project ends with the completion of Scene 5 of the no-frills version of Config File Setup. Because of the amount of time and effort no-frills CFS is taking me to complete, Tier 6 will include working on release version elements, even though I haven't yet finished no-frills CFS.

10/21/2000 - I finished rendering the first of four parts of Scene 4 of Config File Setup of BJQ2T this morning. In order to prevent myself from falling behind in my schedule again, I've decided to cut back a little on the no-frills version of CFS. Only the first part of Scene 4 will be included in the no-frills version; the other three sections will be added in later versions of the episode during future tiers. I've also been forced to postpone the design of the shuttlecar station for the second week in a row. I now have only 11 days until the end of the month and the end of Tier 5 to finish the last three of seven scenes in CFS, and whatever doesn't get finished before then with be worked on during future tiers.

10/16/2000 - And now for the most significant news this site has seen since its creation just over a year ago. The Infinite World Of Wonder is now a part of the Gamespy Network, and under Polycount's umbrella. (You might have noticed the banners by now.) Because of the bandwidth they're giving me, and because of their lack of the bullshit policy I mentioned about Fortunecity, I am now able to show you those first three scenes from the Config File Setup episode of Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics. The construction of Scene 4 of BJQ2T is going slower than I'd like it to, so I might have to alter my plans for the future tiers, but when it's done, I think you'll appreciate the work I'm putting into it. I'm also changing the modeling style of BJQ2T, basing it on the cartoon TV show The Critic.

10/2/2000 - This is my third update in three days, and while no significant changes have been made to IWOW since yesterday, I felt the need make this announcement anyway. The Infinite World Of Wonder is now officially one year old, and to celebrate, I've spent the past six hours creating the lovely piece of eye candy you see at the top of the page. That is all. 

10/1/2000 - With the completion of the third scene of the no-frills CFS movie of my BJQ2T project, Tier 4 is finally vanquished. Hopefully, my server problem will be fixed soon, so I can show you what I've got so far on the project. On to Tier 5 I go!

9/29/2000 - I've been forced to limit this month's completion of the no-frills version of the Config File Setup episode of Brandy And Jatoten's Quake2 Tactics to only the first three scenes. Unfortunately, I haven't yet found another web hosting service to take the place of Fortunecity, but I hope to take care of that by the end of the month.

9/23/2000 - It's been nearly a month since the last update of IWOW, and I spent most of this time working on the draft version of the Config File Setup episode of Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics. Due to the unexpected level of difficulty of this assignment, I've been forced to postpone the other two tasks in Tier 4 to a later tier. I started school at Bergen Community College for the fall a couple weeks ago, and decided to make the CFS movie my school project, which puts additional pressure on getting this damn thing done. And now some more bad news. Fortunecity now prohibits the linking of movie files on their servers from outside pages, which is a total bullshit policy IMHO. So until I find another free webspace provider, we'll have to get by without being able to view movies on this site. :( This is especially bad timing, since I finished the first scene of CFS v0.1 today. So, until the next update, later days.

8/26/2000 - This is a minor update, so I'll make it quick. I updated the links section of IWOW, and it now includes two banners. That is all.

8/25/2000 - Tier 3 of the Brandy And Jatoten's Quake 2 Tactics project is now officially completed. Even better is the fact that I finished it with six days to spare.

8/16/2000 - I hope you're enjoying the new look of the front page. The two souls you see in the big pic above is yours truly holding Candystick, and my adopted elfin daughter, Brandy Hothiegh, holding her trusty shotgun, Mr. Bang. In other news, I got my sister to agree to play the voice for Brandy in my BJQ2T project. I'm also done with the script of the first movie of the project.
