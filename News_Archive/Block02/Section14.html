News Archive - Section 14

1/11/2016 - On this first update of Creamy's Wonderland of the year, I can finally announce that all 20 animations of the Austavin_Quake 2_PPM are finished. On the Austavin page under Games / Quake 2 / PPMs, I've posted previews of the three standing death animations. I've also written a quick report of my efforts to reduce the model's patch count before I export it into a series of 3ds files, so that I can continue work on the model in Quake Model Editor.

12/29/2015 - All five crouching animations of the Austavin_Quake 2_PPM are now completed. Check out previews of these animations on the Austavin page under Games / Quake 2 / PPMs.

12/17/2015 - Merely two days after the previous site update, I already have additional progress on the Austavin_Quake 2_PPM to report. All five gesture animations are now completed, and can be previewed on the Austavin page under Games / Quake 2 / PPMs.

12/15/2015 - All three Standing Pain animations and the Jump animation of the Austavin_Quake 2_PPM are now completed. Head on down to the Austavin page under Games / Quake 2 / PPMs to get a feel for what they will look like in the game.

11/18/2015 - The Run and Standing Attack animations of the Austavin_Quake 2_PPM are now completed. You can watch previews of both of these animations on the Austavin page under Games / Quake 2 / PPMs.

10/23/2015 - Just a small update this time. Nearly two weeks ago, I completed the Stance animation for my new Austavin_Quake 2_PPM. You can find this short clip of the animation in the Games / Quake 2 section of Creamy's Wonderland.

9/22/2015 - Since May of this year, I've been working on my fourth Quake 2_PPM. This model is one of Austavin, who is the online persona of my 15 year old nephew Austin Luke, and you can check out its progress on the latest page in the PPMs section under Games / Quake 2. Also, I finished my very own edit of the MUGEN character Luigi by Warner, N64Mario and TMasta three days ago. This edit is now available on the Characters page under Games / MUGEN.

5/13/2015 - There is now a new sub-section under CGF / VG Longplays called Earthbound, where I play the SNES game of the same name for the first time.

1/24/2015 - It's been over eight months since Creamy's Wonderland's last update, but I've made some pretty major changes that I think make it worth the wait. First off, there is a new but belated section to Live Events / QuakeCon that describes my 2014 trip to the QuakeCon in Dallas, Texas. Also, if you've noticed that my Creamy Goodness Films videos on YouTube have been offline for the past several months, that's because I deleted my YouTube account due to my frustration with the detremental changes the site had been making over the past few years. However, I started a new account on another video sharing site known as ZippCast, and I've uploaded most of my CGF videos there.

> Finally, as promised in the site's previous update, I've taken down Boss Brandy after it had been the front page mural for nearly three and a half years. The new mural replacing it is called End Of An Era, and it depicts the closing of my YouTube account.

5/20/2014 - I've got a rather major update for you today. This time around, I've started a new Lifebar section under Games / MUGEN, and it currently only features the Radio Lifebar. Get it while it's hot!

> By the way, if you're familiar with the way I run this site, I usually change the front page mural whenever I purge the news updates on the page every ten updates. However, I don't have a new mural to show this time around, but I will do everything I can to get one up on the next update.
